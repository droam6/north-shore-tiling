What We Offer

Sydney's Trusted Tiling Experts

At North Shore Tiling, we deliver exceptional tiling solutions that combine precision craftsmanship with premium materials. Whether you're renovating a bathroom, updating your kitchen, or installing new floor tiles, our experienced team ensures a flawless finish every time.

We understand that quality tiling is an investment in your property. That's why we use only the best materials and techniques, ensuring your tiles not only look stunning but stand the test of time.

  • Bathroom Tiling Complete bathroom transformations including walls, floors, and shower recesses with certified waterproofing.
  • Kitchen Splashbacks Stylish and functional splashback installations to protect and enhance your kitchen space.
  • Floor Tiling Durable floor tile installations for living areas, hallways, and high-traffic zones.
  • Outdoor & Pool Areas Weather-resistant outdoor tiling for patios, pool surrounds, and alfresco areas.
  • Waterproofing Certified waterproofing membrane installation to Australian standards.
