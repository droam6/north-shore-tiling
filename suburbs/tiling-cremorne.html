Cremorne Tiling

Thoughtful Tiling for Cremorne's Charming Properties

Cremorne is a harbour suburb with a character all its own, tucked between the busier centres of Neutral Bay and Mosman with a quiet, village-like feel that belies its proximity to the Sydney CBD. The suburb's streetscape is a picturesque blend of Federation-era homes with decorative timber detailing, solid inter-war brick apartments, mid-century walk-ups and pockets of newer boutique developments. Properties here tend to be well-maintained and lovingly updated by owners who appreciate the suburb's relaxed coastal atmosphere and want their homes to reflect that same sense of understated quality. North Shore Tiling has become a preferred tiling partner for Cremorne residents who want renovations done right.

Bathroom modernisation is by far the most common tiling request we receive from Cremorne homeowners. Many of the suburb's apartments and houses still have original or first-renovation bathrooms from the 1970s, 1980s or early 1990s, complete with small, dated tiles, discoloured grout and waterproofing that may no longer be adequate. Our team approaches each Cremorne bathroom project as an opportunity to completely reimagine the space, stripping it back to allow for proper waterproofing, reconfiguring layouts where practical and installing tiles that bring warmth, light and a contemporary sensibility to the room.

Boutique Renovations with Personal Attention

What sets Cremorne renovation projects apart from those in larger, more commercially driven suburbs is the personal, boutique nature of the work. Homeowners here are typically hands-on and involved in every decision, from selecting the exact shade of grout to choosing the orientation of each tile. We welcome this level of engagement and treat each project as a collaborative process. Our tilers take the time to lay out tiles dry before installation, discuss pattern alignment with homeowners and make adjustments on site to ensure the finished result matches the vision. This attention to detail is what keeps Cremorne clients coming back and referring us to their neighbours.

Kitchen Splashbacks and Living Area Floors

Beyond bathrooms, Cremorne homeowners are increasingly investing in kitchen splashback upgrades and living area floor tiling as part of broader renovation projects. A fresh splashback in textured subway tiles, a geometric pattern or a natural stone finish can completely revitalise a kitchen, while extending floor tiles from the kitchen through to living and dining areas creates a seamless, open-plan flow that suits the way Cremorne families live today.
