Lindfield Tiling

Quality Tiling Services in Lindfield's Renovation-Active Community

Lindfield has long been one of the North Shore's most charming and desirable suburbs. Its village atmosphere, anchored by the collection of boutique shops and cafes along Lindfield Avenue near the train station, gives the area a warmth and community feel that larger commercial centres lack. The residential streets, including Balfour Street, Havilah Road and Tryon Road, feature an appealing mix of original California bungalows, post-war homes and contemporary new builds. This diversity makes Lindfield one of the most renovation-active suburbs on the upper North Shore, and North Shore Tiling is proud to be the local tiling partner for many of these transformations.

Renovation in Lindfield often starts with the bathroom. Whether it is a modest update to a family bathroom in a 1960s brick home or a complete luxury renovation in a newly subdivided property, our tiling team brings the same level of expertise and attention to detail. We begin every project with a thorough site assessment, checking substrate conditions, existing waterproofing and plumbing access before providing a detailed quote. This transparency means there are no surprises once the work begins, a quality that Lindfield homeowners particularly appreciate.

Bathroom Retiling and Waterproofing

Older Lindfield homes frequently have bathrooms where the waterproofing has reached the end of its effective life. Telltale signs include bubbling paint on the other side of the wall, musty odours and loose tiles. Our team strips the area back to the substrate, makes any necessary repairs, applies a new waterproof membrane to Australian Standards and then installs your new tiles. We work with all tile types, from classic white ceramics and penny-round mosaics to large-format rectified porcelain that gives a clean, modern look. Kitchen and Laundry Tiling

Lindfield kitchens benefit from thoughtfully chosen splashbacks that tie together the room's design. We install everything from classic subway tile in herringbone patterns to full-height stone-look porcelain panels. Our tilers take particular care with edge finishing, ensuring neat silicone lines where tiles meet benchtops and window frames. Laundry rooms are another area where quality tiling improves both function and aesthetics. Slip-resistant floor tiles and easy-clean wall tiles make the laundry a more pleasant and practical space.
